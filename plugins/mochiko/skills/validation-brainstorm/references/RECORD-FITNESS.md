# Standalone-Record Fitness

The pressure-tester's final checklist over `record.md`. The record is the session's only
artifact — grading surface, audit trail, and deliverable in one — so it must stand entirely
on its own. Cite evidence from the record for every item; an item without cited evidence is
unchecked, and any unchecked item blocks `ready`.

- [ ] **Self-contained** — readable without the conversation: a cold reader can reconstruct
      what was decided, why, and what was rejected, from this file alone
- [ ] **Decisions attackable** — every decision carries a rationale concrete enough to argue
      with; no bare assertions
- [ ] **Challenge trail present** — each decision shows its challenge(s) and resolution, or
      is visibly marked as never challenged (which the coverage audit then weighs)
- [ ] **Confidence marks honest** — `Contested` on overruled challenges, `Assumed` on
      recommendation-led adoptions never explicitly confirmed, `Deferred`/`Unsure` where true;
      no silent upgrades to `Confident`
- [ ] **Rejected roads recorded** — the alternatives considered are named with why they lost,
      so a future reader does not re-derive them
- [ ] **Honest about the open** — undecided threads, provisional rulings, and revisit
      triggers are listed as open, not absorbed into settled prose
- [ ] **Provenance stated** — who/what produced and pressure-tested the record, and when,
      so a consumer can tell what review it survived
